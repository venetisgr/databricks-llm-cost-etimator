⚠ Important Estimation Notes
→⚠ Worst case is 512 tokens / call, which is the maximum output token limit set for these models — actual average likely 200–400; even the difference of 100 tokens saves significantly at 6,700+ calls/day.
→Databricks bills in DBUs not USD. The $0.07/DBU rate is the public pay-per-token promotional rate. Enterprise contracts and committed-use discounts (1 or 3 year) can lower this by up to 37%.
→The system prompt (944 tokens) is applied per call. At baseline that alone costs ~6.3M input tokens/day — trimming even 200 words saves ~1.7M tokens/day.
→Call transcript tokenization: English averages 0.75 words/token, but spoken language with jargon, numbers, or non-English content may tokenize at 1.2–1.5× the word count.
→At this volume, Databricks recommends Provisioned Throughput over pay-per-token — it offers guaranteed capacity and can be more economical for high, sustained call rates.
